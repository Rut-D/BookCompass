The process

Brewed in three steps just for you

Think of it as ordering off menu. You describe your craving, we let it steep, and your shelf shows up ready to sip.

01

Describe

Tell us the trope, the mood, the setting — "grumpy sunshine," "cozy witchy autumn," anything at all.

02

Steep

We match your words against a shelf of favorites, letting the right stories rise to the top.

03

Sip your list

Your recommendations land as little library cards — title, blurb, and a bean rating to trust.
